About

A practical accessibility advocate for everyday product teams.

My work focuses on turning inclusive design goals into durable front-end systems: clear structure, predictable interaction, readable content, and careful testing.

  • Focus: semantic HTML, responsive CSS, accessible forms, and design-system implementation.
  • Process: prototype, validate with keyboard and screen-reader heuristics, then harden with automated checks.
  • Values: clarity, maintainability, measurable accessibility, and plain-language collaboration.
